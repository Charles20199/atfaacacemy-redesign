Invest in a Teen's Financial Future

ATFA's groundbreaking internship program takes financial education to the next level, giving talented teens the opportunity to manage real investments under expert guidance.

How Our Program Works

1

Paper Trading Competition

Teens participate in our simulated stock trading competition, learning market fundamentals without financial risk.

2

Skill Development

Participants develop investment strategies, learn market analysis, and compete against their peers.

3

Champion Selection

The winner demonstrates superior trading skills, risk management, and investment knowledge.

4

Real Money Management

Our champion graduates to managing a $1,000 real investment account with dedicated mentorship support.
